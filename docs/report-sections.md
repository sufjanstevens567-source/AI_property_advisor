# Report Sections Specification

This specification outlines the structure of the final exported report (Phase 8 & 9).

## 1. Executive Summary
- Verdict (e.g., Conditional Buy, Marginal, Avoid).
- High-level metrics: Asking Price, CF at 30%, Economic ROI, Stress Resilience Category, Data Quality Score.

## 2. Property & Listing Facts
- Address, beds, baths, size (m²), BER, parking, service charge.
- Data Quality summary (highlights what is estimated vs. confirmed).

## 3. Financial Model (Central Case: 30% Tax)
- **Acquisition Breakdown:** Price, stamp duty, legal, refurb, BER upgrade, total cost, mortgage required.
- **Operating Costs:** Service charge, maintenance, insurance, accounting, letting, capex.
- **Returns:** Base rent, vacancy adjustment, estimated tax, monthly/annual CF, Economic ROI.

## 4. Rent Evidence & Confidence
- Table of comparables (Address, Rent, Tier A-D).
- Conclusion on Market vs. Legally Achievable rent.
- Rent Sensitivity Table (Conservative / Base / Strong).

## 5. Stress Tests & Sensitivity
- Highlights from the 10 scenarios.
- Focus on Scenario #9 (Combined Downside) and #10 (Severe Downside).
- Service Charge Sensitivity (if SC was estimated).

## 6. Qualitative Risk Scoring
- Visual breakdown of the 16 dimensions (1-10).
- Building/OMC Risk Table (Historical vs. Current).
- BER / Energy Efficiency Risk notes.

## 7. Negotiation Strategy
- Price Sensitivity Table (Asking, Target, Stretch, Walk-away).
- Negotiation Rationale & Escalation Plan.
- Due Diligence Levers (What findings increase or decrease the offer).
- Agent Script template.

## 8. Due Diligence / "What I Would Do Next"
- Actionable next steps (e.g., "Request OMC pack", "Verify RTB registration").
- Go / No-Go triggers for the specific property.
