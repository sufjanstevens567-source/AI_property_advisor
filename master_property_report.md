## Section 3: Assumptions and Formula Definitions

**Acquisition cost:**
```
Total acquisition cost = purchase price + stamp duty (1%) + legal/setup (тВм8,500 default)
                       + BER upgrade (if applicable) + refurbishment (if applicable)
Mortgage = total acquisition cost тИТ тВм300,000 cash
```

**Monthly mortgage:** M = P ├Ч r ├Ч (1+r)^300 / ((1+r)^300 тИТ 1), where r = annual rate / 12. Year-1 interest/principal computed by iterating 12 monthly amortisation steps.

**Cash flow:**
```
Vacancy-adjusted rent = monthly rent x 11 (base: 1 month vacancy)
Tax deductibles       = yr-1 mortgage interest + SC + insurance + maintenance + accounting
Taxable rental profit = vacancy-adjusted rent тИТ deductibles (floored at zero)
Annual after-tax CF   = vacancy-adjusted rent тИТ all op costs тИТ estimated tax тИТ annual mortgage
Economic return       = annual after-tax CF + year-1 principal repayment
Economic ROI          = economic return / тВм300,000
```
